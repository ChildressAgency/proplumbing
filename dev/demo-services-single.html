Sewer and Septic Services In Fredericksburg and King George, VA

Doesn't it always seem like the septic acts up when you have company in town for the weekend. We know how much of a headache that can be and we are here to help.

When it comes to most sewer and drain problems, we are the solution. From simple clogs, sewer, effluent, and grinder pumps, broken or damaged pipes, broken tees in the spetic tank, and real estate inspections, we can help.

The technicians at PPSI are professionals at diagnosing and resolving your sewer and drain problems. We have many years of experience working on sewage, effluent, and grinder pump systems, underground piping, and control boxes.

If you have a clogged sewer or an issue with your septic tank, we have that covered too. We have invested in some of the best equipment to resolve your problems from various sizes of sewer and drain snakes, jetting equipment, and state of the art sewer video inspection equipment.

We ca see what is going on inside your sewer pipe, and with our locating equipment, we can pinpoint where the problem is located in your yard or under the sidewalk or driveway.
